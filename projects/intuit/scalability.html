Sage

Sage allows users to upgrade with various offerings allowing users to choose a solution that fits their needs. Users have the flexibility to adapt to demand. Sage does offer integrations with commonly used business applications, allowing users to capture additional data.

QuickBooks

QuickBooks has two core offerings that overlap functionality, but users can switch between one or the other to quickly increase or decrease capability and capacity as needed. QuickBooks Enterprise integrates with other commonly used applications like Salesforce, DocuSign, PayPal and ecommerce apps like Shopify. These integrations remove the need to recapture or export data during times of rapid growth.
