Sage

Sage customers can contact customer support directly with a toll-free number or access support services via Sage Support Central.

Sage Support Central offers; Learn; Online courses from Sage University, Solve; 24/7 access to a support knowledgebase, Inquire; 24/7 forum to tap industry peers and certified partners for advice, tips, and share experiences, and Contact; Access additional online support content, submit an online case, or chat with Sage.

Sage 50Cloud* includes:

  • Unlimited access to North American-based phone support.
  • Unlimited online chat support.
  • Knowledgebase resource library with 24/7 access to support content.
  • Sage City 24/7 online community of industry peers, certified partners and Sage pros.

*Included with all three Sage 50Cloud pricing plans: Pro, Premium, and Quantum.

QuickBooks

QuickBooks Enterprise and QuickBooks Advanced Online subscriptions includes Priority Circle, a premium support service with exclusive features.

Priority Circle grants access to premium training and supports features:

  • A dedicated account team
  • On-demand QuickBooks training
  • 24/7 phone or chat with premium care specialists.

QuickBooks support page provides several non-premium services features: A quick search, Select a product–to customize support, Top three topics–expands to reveal more topics, Learn QuickBooks–featuring video tutorials and webinars, Ask the Community, What’s new, and Resources; Find an expert–find a certified advisor, bookkeeper, or accountant, Resource center–articles and guides, and QuickBooks blog.
