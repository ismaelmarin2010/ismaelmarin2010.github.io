Sage

Sage Intacct Marketplace and Sage Cloud Marketplace provides their users with native solutions that integrate over 200 business applications. Quick links in both marketplaces are grouped by Category and Industry.

Sage Intacct Marketplace has an additional “Groupings” category with links for:

  • Direct Integrations
  • 3rd Party Connectors
  • Built by Sage Intacct Corporation
  • Built on Sage Intacct Platform

QuickBooks

QuickBooks has two core offerings that overlap functionality, but users can switch between one or the other to quickly increase or decrease capability and capacity as needed. QuickBooks Enterprise integrates with other commonly used applications like Salesforce, DocuSign, PayPal and ecommerce apps like Shopify. These integrations remove the need to recapture or export data during times of rapid growth.
