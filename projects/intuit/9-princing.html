Sage

Sage Intacct requires an annual subscription and there is no cost specified on their site. Customers fill out a pricing request form to begin the process of customizing a pricing plan.



Sage 50Cloud offers a monthly and annual subscription plan with three base tiers:

Pro Accounting $340 / year. 1 user.

Premium Accounting $510 / year. 1-5 users.

Quantum Accounting $842 / year

Additional add-ons and applications may increase cost.

Sage is currently offering new customers 40% off their first year.

QuickBooks

QuickBooks online Advanced is a monthly subscription plan, $150 / month for up to 25 users. Additional services like QuickBooks Payroll and QuickBooks Time increase the monthly fee.

QuickBooks is currently offering a free 30-day trial or 50% off the first three months.

QuickBooks Enterprise offers three subscription plans: Gold, Platinum, and Diamond.

The Gold and Platinum plans are annual subscriptions.

Gold: $1489.50 / year. 1 user –$5042.40 / year for 40 users.

Platinum: $1889.50 / year. 1 user –$5272.40 / year for 40 users.

The Diamond plan offers monthly and annual subscriptions: $3618.00 / year. 1 user –$8488.80 / year for 40 users.

QuickBooks is offering a 10% off the first year on all plans for 1-4 users and 40% off for 5-40 users.
