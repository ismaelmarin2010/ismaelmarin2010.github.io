Sage

Sage’s site claims Sage Intacct users “can be up and running in as few as 60 days.” Reviewers note implementation for Sage Intacct or Sage 50cloud is about 90 days.

Expect factors like number of users, add-ons, data migration, and third-party integrations to impact your timeline.

QuickBooks

QuickBooks does not give a specific range for implementing QuickBooks Advanced or QuickBooks Enterprise. Reviewers noted the average experience is six to eight weeks to implement QuickBooks.

As with Sage, factors like number of users, add-ons, data migration, and third-party integrations will impact your timeline.
